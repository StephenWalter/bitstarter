In 2011, 715,373 motor vehicles with a combined value of more than $4.3 billion were reported stolen in the US, according to an offical FBI Report. That means on average a vehicle is stolen every 44 seconds. And only 11 percent of these thefts were every cleared by arrests or other means.

In Europe the picture is similar, with more than 300,000 vehicles stolen every year. Vehicle related theft in the UK accounts for 14 percent of all crimes, with more that 70 percent of vehicles never being seen again.

So how can you prevent yourself and your vehicle becoming just part of those statistics?

Well you could buy one of the existing security and vehicle offerings available on the market, but this could end up costing around $1000 for a complete package, plus the on-going subscriptions fees. This also locks you into the suppliers proprietory hardware and software.

Or you could buy Autodroid the new feature rich and smart alternative. For example, unlike other products Autodroid permits you to 'ring fence' a specific area and will SMS you if that perimeter is breached.

Why Android? Quite simply because it's the most open, flexible, cost effective and powerful eco-system currently available.

All this means that we can offer a device that will offer safety and security at an affordable price now. And one that is also future proofed to accommodate your demands and requirements for tomorrow.

And if you're not sure of that this small device can prove all that you need, just ask the scientists at Surrey Satellite Technology Ltd (SSTL) that recently launched the first Android powered satellite into Earth orbit.
